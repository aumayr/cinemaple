Become a Host or an Ambassador

With Cinemaple, you can even organize your own Movie Nights.

  • Become a Host and organize Movie Nights at your place. To become a Host for a location which is not registered yet, write us at contact@cinemaple.com
  • Once you have become a Host, you can create a new Movie Night by logging in and going to Admin Actions > Add Movie Night.
  • You can also become an Ambassador of a given Location. Just ask the Hosts to send you an activation key.

Invite your friends to Cinemaple

Movie Night Hosts can designate Ambassadors, who can invite their friends to Cinemaple, so they can attend Movie Nights at the Host's location.

Hosts and Ambassadors will be provided a unique invitation link, which can be used to invite new users to the Hosts's location.
