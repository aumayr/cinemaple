Welcome to Cinemaple !

We are excited to have you on board! Cinemaple is a platform to facilitate the organization of Movie Nights and share good times with your friends. Here is some information on how to use Cinemaple:

Attend Movie Nights in your area

With Cinemaple, you can attend Movie Nights organized by your friends. It's very simple:

Log into Cinemaple and go to Current Movie Nights.

Register and vote for your favorite movie(s).

Tell your hosts which crepe toppings/food you are bringing with you.

Register for the Current Movie Night

Become a Host or an Ambassador

With Cinemaple, you can even organize your own Movie Nights.

Become a host and organize Movie Nights at your place. To become a host for a location which is not registered yet, write us at contact@cinemaple.com

Once you have become a host, you can create a new Movie Night by logging in and going to Admin Actions > Add Movie Night.

You can also become an Ambassador of a given Location. Just ask the Hosts to send you an activation key.

Invite your friends to Cinemaple.

I want to invite my friends to Cinemaple

Hosts and Ambassadors can invite their friends to Cinemaple, so they can attend Movie Nights at the Host's location.

Hosts and Ambassadors can generate activation keys by logging in and going to their User Info.

Any more questions?

We wrote a FAQ section to help you navigate Cinemaple. Otherwise, feel free to shoot us an email at contact@cinemaple.com. We will do our very best to answer promptly.
