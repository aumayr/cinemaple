WHAT IS CINEMAPLE?

Toni Servillo in La Grande Bellezza (Paolo Sorrentino, 2013)

Cinemaple is an online-platform designed for organizing Movie Nights with your friends, share good times and a common passion for the art of cinema. It was developped as an open-source project, which means that its code is public and shared to whoever would like to use. However, don't worry, your data is protected (see section below).

Cinemaple attempts to provide a standalone solution to organize Movie Nights, while not involving social networks.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Cinemaple is a userfriendly solution for organizing, sharing, and attending Movie Nights.

Users who want to organize their own Movie Nights are Hosts of a Location. There can be several Hosts per Location. Hosts can send invitation links to their friends, who then register are Cinemaple's Users. They are linked to the Location of the Host who invited them.

The Host of a Location can organize a Movie Night at his/her Location. A Movie Night consists in a theme, a short description, and a selection of movies. The Guests are invited to the Movie Night and get to vote for their favorite movie(s). Guests respond to the Host invitation.

A word on the voting system:

Once a movie has been selected, the Guests gather at the Host's location and share good times while watching the chosen movie, simple as that!
